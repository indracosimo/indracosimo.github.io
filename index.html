Oh hey, what's goin' on? My name is Indra and I live in Stockholm, Sweden. I am a game programmer; however, my passion for games lies in design. One of my favorite aspects of development is discussing what the game is going to be and how design decisions influence each other. When it comes to playing games or discussing projects, I feel like I have a good eye for mechanics, game loops that play into each other, and how to make something fun and interesting. I like games that are challenging, I am the type of gamer who has fun learning from my failures and using that knowledge to my advantage. I carry this same mentality in game development and programming. My dream is to have my own studio where I can be more of a game director. When it comes to game development and programming, I see myself as a bit of an all-arounder. I think my biggest strength is recognizing the individual strengths of my teammates, as well as the ability to keep everyone on the same page. Breaking a problem down into smaller bite-sized chunks is the best way to code and manage!
